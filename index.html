A new Linux distro with flexibility, power, and style.

About Chai Linux

Chai Linux is a lightweight, fast, and customizable Linux distribution. It's designed to be simple yet powerful. Whether you're a developer or a casual user, Chai Linux aims to provide a perfect balance of stability and performance.

Supported Architectures

  • x86_64 - 64-bit systems
  • x86 - 32-bit systems
  • ARMv8 - ARM architecture for mobile and embedded devices

Chai Linux Base Distributions

Chai Linux can be based on a variety of upstream distributions. Right now, we are exploring the following:

  • Debian - The stability and vast package availability.
  • Gentoo - For those who want complete control and optimization.
  • Slackware - A classic approach with simplicity and minimalism.
  • AntiX - A lightweight option for older hardware.

Future Plans

We are excited about the possibility of creating a text-based installer that will make installing Chai Linux even easier and more customizable. The installer will guide users through a simple and intuitive process, helping them install Chai Linux on a wide variety of systems.

Note: Chai Linux is currently still in development. It is an idea that we're working on, and the project is not yet released. Stay tuned for updates!
