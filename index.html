All About Coffee

What is Coffee?

Coffee is a brewed beverage made from roasted coffee beans, the seeds of berries from the Coffea plant. It's one of the most popular drinks worldwide and has a long history dating back centuries.

Types of Coffee

There are many different types of coffee, from espresso and cappuccino to lattes and cold brew. Each type has its own unique preparation method and flavor profile!

  • Espresso
  • Cappuccino
  • Latte
  • Americano
  • Cold Brew
